# Executive Presentation

Build presentation material for managers and customers.

## Rules

- Start from a realistic business case.
- Show before/after when claiming improvement.
- Use evidence screenshots when available.
- Keep risks polite and actionable.
- Put sensitive concerns in appendix or Q&A material.
